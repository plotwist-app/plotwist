# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 14, 2026

Plotwist ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and iOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect your **email address** and **display name**. This information is necessary to provide our services.

### User Preferences
We store your content preferences (genres, content types, streaming services, and region) to personalize your experience. These preferences are stored locally on your device and synced with your account.

### Usage Data
We use **PostHog** to collect anonymous analytics data to improve our services, such as:
- Pages and screens visited
- Features used (searches, reviews, list actions)
- App performance metrics
- Device type and operating system

This data is **anonymous** and cannot be used to identify you personally.

### Content Data
We store information about movies, series, and other content you add to your lists, reviews you write, and ratings you provide.

## How We Use Your Information

- **Provide our services:** Manage your account, lists, reviews, and preferences.
- **Personalize your experience:** Show content recommendations based on your preferences.
- **Improve our services:** Analyze anonymous usage patterns to fix bugs and improve features.
- **Communicate with you:** Respond to feedback and support requests.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

- **TMDB (The Movie Database):** We use TMDB's API to provide movie and TV show data. No personal data is sent to TMDB.
- **PostHog:** Anonymous analytics data is processed by PostHog. See [PostHog's Privacy Policy](https://posthog.com/privacy).
- **Legal requirements:** If required by law or to protect our rights.

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices. We use encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all data transfers.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Update** your account information
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Export** your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **dev@plotwist.app**.

## Children's Privacy

Plotwist is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at **dev@plotwist.app**.
